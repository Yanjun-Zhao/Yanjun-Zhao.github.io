Yanjun Zhao

Ph.D. Student · University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign
Advised by Prof. Jingrui He, work closely with Prof. Hanghang Tong

My research focuses on LLM/VLM agent, with an emphasis on self-improving systems, long-horizon memory and reasoning. Before joining UIUC, I earned my bachelor's and master's degrees from Xi'an Jiaotong University, where I worked closely with Prof. Yi Qian and Prof. Haishan Ye.

Email Scholar GitHub

Open to research discussions and collaborations.

  • Agentic Systems: long-horizon agents, self-evolving systems
  • LLM Reasoning: long context reasoning, post training
  • Optimization: zeroth-order optimization for fine-tuning LLM
  • Application: data mining, time series forecasting
